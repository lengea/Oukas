Study now, Pay later Model

With our model, students can focus on their studies
while we and our partners take care of the tuition fees.
Once the student graduates, he/she will work for their
supported partner for a set period of time until the
tuition fee is paid back in full.

Students have the options to repay from 10% to 50%
of their monthly salary to best suit their needs.

How it works:

Raksa Sok

Background:
Age: 19
Occupation: Student

Context:
Just graduated from
high-school and currently looking
for a university.

Situation: Raksa wants to complete her higher-level education.
Problem: Her family cannot afford to pay for her tuition fees and do not want to risk of
losing their home for a loan.
Solution: Oukas (Study now, Pay later Model)
Raksa takes a loan of $2500 to complete her 4 years study in accounting supported by CMG (partner)
4 years later (after graduating), Raksa goes and work for CMG with a monthly salary of $350
Repayment option: 50% cut from her monthly income
Repayment plan: $175 per month back to CMG for a duration of 14 months.
